Hi there, I’m Sangeeta.

I create thoughtful experiences that are built for real users to solve real problems through our designs. All in all, I believe that as a designer, I have a mission to genuinely care for the people I design for. Also, there is a lot of information available regarding any kind of plants so the user can grow their knowledge.

Growing up, whenever I thought about my dream, I always felt, “I want to be an artist”. I was constantly immersed in creative activities - drawing, painting, and designing. After completing my graduate, technology started to become a big part of my life and my interest in the constantly evolving digital world continued to grow.

Previously, I worked as a Web Designer and my primary role was to work on the Website Design and Layout Design. Then I joined as a UI/UX Designer and I worked on several module designs, and mobile responsive design.

My experiences in these roles have sharpened my skills in digital design, working in a fast-paced, high-pressure environment, empathizing with people, doing research and analysis, identifying goals and needs, and efficiently finding solutions to a broad variety of user pain points.

How did I get into UX? 

Something that I truly enjoyed throughout all the different experiences I’ve had is the meaningful interactions with the people that I work with. I chose to pursue this new path into UX Design where I could utilize both of my passions in a new purposeful way. I want to explore design through the emotional, functional, and aesthetic spectrums and have the opportunity to make a positive impact on people’s lives.
